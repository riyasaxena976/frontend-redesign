## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Open in Browser**
   - Open `index.html` in any modern web browser

2. **Navigate Pages**
   - Use navbar menu to navigate between pages
   - All links are fully functional

3. **Switch Themes**
   - Click moon/sun icon in navbar
   - Your preference will be saved

4. **Interact with Forms**
   - Registration popup appears on page load
   - Book demo form and contact form are functional

5. **View Gallery**
   - Click on gallery images to view in lightbox
   - Zoom and navigation available
