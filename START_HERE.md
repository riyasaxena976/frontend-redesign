## 💡 Common Customizations

### Change Phone Number
Search for `+919876543210` and replace with your number.
Do this in all HTML files.

### Change Email
Search for `info@cncinstitute.com` and replace with your email.

### Change Location
Search for `New Delhi, India` and replace with your city.

### Change Course Prices
Open `courses.html` and update prices for each course.

Open `styles.css` and change these lines:
```css

```
